I am currently a first-year PhD student at UC Riverside. Before that, I completed my bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at NIT Silchar, India. I moved to UC Riverside for my master’s in 2022, specializing in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. I completed my master’s project under the supervision of Professor Gregory Ver Steeg, and I continue my research under his guidance.

In addition to my research, I have professional experience as a software engineer in the information technology industry, where I worked on different tech stacks. I believe that to be a good researcher in computer science, one must first be a good computer engineer, solving real-world problems effectively.

I also enjoy building software in my spare time. One of my key side projects is collaborating with Professor Andy Ridgewell to modernize his numerical Fortran-based model, Genie.
